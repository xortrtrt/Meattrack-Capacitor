Know Our Story

Batangas Premium grew from a pandemic-era food idea into a meat processing and distribution business with thousands of sellers.

From Engineers to Entrepreneurs

Batangas Premium was founded on June 22, 2020, by Nicko Jay Landicho and Weng Opena, two mechanical engineers with backgrounds in major projects including Okada Manila, IKEA, and UCC Cafes. When COVID-19 lockdowns disrupted their careers, they pivoted to a new vision: quality frozen meat products.

Starting with skinless longganisa and 2 employees, the company grew into a trusted meat processing and distribution brand serving active distributors across Luzon and Visayas. Today, commercial equipment and dedicated teams support a broader product line.

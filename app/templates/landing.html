From Engineers to Entrepreneurs

Batangas Premium was founded on June 22, 2020, by Nicko Jay Landicho and Weng Opena. The brand grew from skinless longganisa into a trusted frozen meat line serving homes, resellers, and local outlets.

The system behind MEATTRACK supports that growth with inventory, sales, reseller ordering, and demand forecasting in one workflow.
